about us

DFB is a self-managed clothing brand based in Argentina.

DONTFLYBABY is an idea, a concept, placed in a homonymous brand through which the individuals who share and/or incorporate this way of perceiving reality seek to materialize it in the world and in the subconscious mind of the rest of the individuals.

The meaning behind the term "DONTFLYBABY" itself is the reproduction of a statement of limiting character that we feel they have tried to convey to us in an imperative way, but returned with a note of satire, in a mocking way if you will, making use of a metaphor in which it is sought to represent the true manifestation of the individual through the act of flying.
